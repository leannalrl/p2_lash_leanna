About Me

Hello, my name is Leanna Lash and here are a few tidbits about me. I am a native Floridian and have lived in Florida my entire life. Growing up in Florida has its perks, like being a few hours from Disney World and some of the best beaches in the United States. It is really the best state to live in other than Hurricane season. I am basically the quintennial Florida girl and even went to the University of Florida for my bachelor’s degree in in Telecommunication: Media and Society and now my master’s degree in Web Design and Online Communications. Go Gators! Currently, I work in the Tampa Bay area for a Non-Profit as their Director of Graphic Design and Multimedia. In the future I would love to combine my passions of photography with web/graphic design and run my own photography/marketing business.
